Chicken Curry

Chicken curry from the Indian subcontinent typically features chicken stewed in a tomato-based sauce seasoned with aromatic spices. This recipe, like many others, calls for curry powder (a spice blend made with coriander, turmeric, cumin, and chili powder).

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 2 lbs Chicken Breast, cut into bite sized chunks
  • 1 cup of Plain Yogurt
  • 3/4 cup of Coconut Milk
  • 1/2 Lemon, juiced
  • 1 Carton of Broth *Optional*
  • Aromatics and Spices
    • 1 Small Onion, diced
    • 2 Garlic Cloves, minced
    • 3 tbsp Curry Powder
    • 1 tsp Ground Cinnamon
    • 1 tsp Paprika
    • 1 Bay Leaf
    • 1/2 tsp Freshly Grated Ginger
    • 1/2 tsp White Sugar
    • 1/2 tsp Cayenne Pepper
    • Salt, to taste

Cooking Instructions

  1. Saute Onions in heated Olive Oil, until lightly browned.
  2. Add in remainder of aromatics and spices, continue to stir until fragrent. *around 2 Minutes*
  3. Add chicken pieces and let cook until slightly browned, add tomato paste and allow to continue to toast. *let toast for ~3 minutes*
  4. Add coconut milk and adjust consistency with broth. Then bring to a boil, reduce heat and let simmer for 20-25 minutes.
  5. Remove from heat. Remove bay leaf, mix in yogurt and adjust acidity to taste with lemon juice.
  6. Serve over Rice or with Bread and Enjoy!
